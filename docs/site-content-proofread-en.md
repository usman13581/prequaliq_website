## products › prequaliqApps › whatIsIt › title

What is a prequalification system?

---

## products › prequaliqApps › whatIsIt › content

In public procurement, buyers need confidence that suppliers meet legal, financial and quality requirements before they are invited to tender. A prequalification system registers suppliers, verifies key information and maintains a pool of approved businesses ready for procurement opportunities.

---

## products › prequaliqApps › whatIsIt › secondary

PrequaliQ Apps combines supplier prequalification with a dynamic procurement system. Swedish procuring authorities can search qualified suppliers by industry, location and capability, then run efficient procurement processes with suppliers that are already reviewed.

---
